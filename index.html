AFROTC Dictionary

Introduction

The purpose of this dictionary is to provide individuals new to AFROTC a general base of terminology often used by members of the program. It is also for friends & family of students in the program who wish to better understand what these terms mean. All of the information found here is typically learned within the first semester that a student joins. Some of the provided information is specific to the University of Hawaii at Manoa AFROTC program, and may not be applicable to other schools.
